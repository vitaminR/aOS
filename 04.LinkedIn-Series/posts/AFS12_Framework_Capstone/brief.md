# AFS12 — A Framework for Every AI Platform Decision

## Topic

Capstone post for the 12-part "Building an AI Platform That Actually Ships" series. Closes the arc by zooming out from Azure AI Foundry to the universal pattern: every managed AI platform is strong at L1-L3, partial at L4, and structurally weak at L5-L7. The {a}OS 7-stratum model is not an Azure tool — it is a decision framework that applies to any managed AI platform. The series started narrow and ended wide.
